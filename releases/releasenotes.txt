# Driver's log

Description
===========
en

Keep track of your car travels and related expenses. Export your log, in a comma separated format, through e-mail for easy import to Excel and similar applications.

sv-SE

Registrera dina bilresor och relaterade utgifter i journalen. Exportera din journal, i ett kommaseparerat format, via e-post för vidare import i Excel eller liknande applikationer.
